Biography

I am a first year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Texas State University focusing on information management. I have a background in applied mathematics and statistics and have interdisciplinary experience in chemistry, public health, and computer vision. Currently, I work as a doctoral research assistant in Dr. Jelena Tesic's Data Lab focusing on community and bias detection in signed networks.
